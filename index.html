What We Do

It is always challenging at the beginning of most startup journeys to raise money without an MVP or Product. Even many angels and VCs expect to see the first product/MVP and some interactions and feedback from users to invest in your startup. Our job at Molten is to give you your MVP or Product without the financial burdens that come with it.

We will brand the product or idea, design and develop the product, help you go live, and still give you post-launch tech support. In special cases, we will assist you in finding and recruiting the right technical team to take over from us when we’re done.



How it Works

1

Tell Us About Your Idea

Fill out the microform, and share details about the project, your budget, timeline and benchmarks. In the early stages of Molten, we will only be able to work with 10 startups every month.

2

Discovery Call

If we think your idea is clear enough, we will have a discovery call with you to better understand the project, business goals, fundraising plans, and your initial timeline.

3

Agreement & Scope

Our team will analyze all that we discussed on the call and create a project scope, budget and timeline. We will share this with you and if we have an agreement, we will move forward with your project.

4

Production

Once we have an agreement, a project manager and a dedicated project team will be assigned to your project, and a slack channel will be created with you and anyone else from your team to join and follow up on updates.

5

Launch & Support

After we’re done with your product, all testings have been done and our QA team have deemed the product ready and your team are satisfied, we will help you launch properly and every project gets between 3 to 12 months of FREE technical support post-launch.

Payment Plan

Our payment structure is straightforward and we do not take equity or stock options in any startup we work with.
(Except in special cases where cash investments are involved.)

Once you get a scope, quote and timeline, you would only be required to pay 35% of the total cost of the project during development, the remaining 65% can either be split and paid monthly/quarterly before you raise or paid in full when you raise X5 of your debt.

Here’s a sample breakdown for easier understanding: If we charge you a total of $30,000 for your product, you would be required to pay $10,500 to build your product and pay $19,500 when you raise an investment above $97,500 ($19,500 x 5).M

The $10,500 you are to pay for product development will then also be split into milestones, so you only pay for work done.
